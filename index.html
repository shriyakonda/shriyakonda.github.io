About Us

WHO WE ARE

DataWorks is a student-run data consulting organisation at UC San Diego. We're a team of data science, computer science, and statistics students who work with real businesses on real data problems — from analytics and reporting to machine learning and pipelines.

We started DataWorks because we saw a gap. Data is one of the fastest-growing fields in the world, but for students, the transition from coursework to industry work is steep. You can know Python and statistics inside out and still struggle to translate that into something a business actually needs.

We wanted to build a space where students could get hands-on industry experience before graduation — working on real projects, with real stakes, and real feedback. At the same time, we wanted to connect students across disciplines who share a passion for data, and give them a community to grow in together.

The jump from school to a data job is one of the hardest transitions in tech. DataWorks exists to make it shorter.
